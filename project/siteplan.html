Site Plan: Naija Food Heritage Hub

Site Name

Name: Naija-foodhub.com

Description: The name reflects a celebration of Nigeria’s diverse culinary traditions and their cultural significance and a showcase of mouth watering delicacies

Site Purpose

The Naija Food Heritage Hub aims to celebrate and preserve Nigeria’s rich culinary traditions by providing a digital platform for authentic recipes, cultural food stories, and local ingredient guides. It offers services like educational content on Nigerian food culture.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: As a Nigerian-American college student, what ingredients or steps do I need to recreate my grandmother’s Egusi soup recipe?. Visit the site to find an authentic recipe with a step-by-step guide and tips on sourcing ingredients like melon seeds
  2. Scenario 2: As a food blogger interested in African cuisine, what is the cultural significance of Jollof Rice in Nigeria? Visit the site to explore the "Food Stories" section for historical context.

Color Schema

The color schema is inspired by Nigeria’s vibrant culture and natural ingredients:

  • #006600 (Dark Green): Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) and navigation bar background to evoke Nigeria’s flourishing landscapes and the green of the national flag.
  • #FFD700 (Golden Yellow): Used for accents (e.g., buttons, links, hover effects) and secondary backgrounds to represent the warmth of Nigerian spices and hospitality.

Typography

The typography is clean and modern, ensuring readability while reflecting cultural vibrancy:

  • Lora (Serif): Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) to convey elegance and tradition, aligning with the heritage theme.
  • Open Sans (Sans-Serif): Used for body text and captions for clarity and appeal, ensuring accessibility across devices.
